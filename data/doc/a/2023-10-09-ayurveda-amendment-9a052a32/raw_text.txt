AN ACT TO AMEND THE AYURVEDA ACT, NO. 31 OF 1961

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Ayurveda (Amendment)

Short title

Act, No. 19 of 2023.

(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended by the substitution for the words commencing from
“FOR  THE  REGISTRATION  OF  AYURVEDA
HOSPITALS” to the words “DISPENSARIES ATTACHED
THERETO;” of the following: -
