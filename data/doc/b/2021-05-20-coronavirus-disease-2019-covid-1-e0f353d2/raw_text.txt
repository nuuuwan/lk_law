CORONAVIRUS DISEASE 2019 (COVID - 19)
(TEMPORARY  PROVISIONS)

A

BILL

to make temporary provisions in relation to situations where persons were
unable to perfom certain actions required by law to be performed within
the prescribed time periods due to Covid - 19 circumstances; to assign
alternative  courts  where  a  court  cannot  function  due  to  Covid  -  19
circumstances; to conduct court proceedings using remote communication
technology  to  facilitate  the  control  of  coronavirus  disease  2019
(Covid - 19); to grant relief in relation to parties to certain contracts who
were   unable   to   perform   contractual   obligations   due   to   Covid - 19
circumstances and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto

AN ACT TO MAKE TEMPORARY PROVISIONS IN RELATION TO SITUATIONS
WHERE PERSONS WERE UNABLE TO PERFOM CERTAIN ACTIONS
REQUIRED BY LAW TO BE PERFORMED WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME
PERIODS DUE TO COVID - 19 CIRCUMSTANCES; TO ASSIGN ALTERNATIVE
COURTS WHERE A COURT CANNOT FUNCTION DUE TO COVID - 19
CIRCUMSTANCES; TO CONDUCT COURT PROCEEDINGS USING REMOTE
COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY TO FACILITATE THE CONTROL OF
CORONAVIRUS DISEASE 2019 (COVID - 19); TO GRANT RELIEF IN
RELATION TO PARTIES TO CERTAIN CONTRACTS WHO WERE UNABLE TO
PERFORM  CONTRACTUAL  OBLIGATIONS  DUE  TO  COVID  -  19
CIRCUMSTANCES AND FOR MATTERS CONNECTED THEREWITH OR
INCIDENTAL THERETO.
