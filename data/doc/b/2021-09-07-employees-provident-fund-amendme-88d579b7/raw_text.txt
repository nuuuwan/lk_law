STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause    2  :  This  clause  amends  section  47  of  the  Employees’  Provident  Fund  Act,
No.  15  of  1958  and  the  legal  effect  of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  exempt  an  international
worker  from  making  any  contribution  under  the  aforesaid  Act  who  is  contributing  to  a
social  security  programme  in  his  own  country  with  which  Sri  Lanka  has  a  social  security
agreement,  for  the  period  as  set  out  in  such  agreement.
