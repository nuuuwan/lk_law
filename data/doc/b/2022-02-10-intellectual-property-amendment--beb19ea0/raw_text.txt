STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

This  Bill  amends  the  Intellectual  Property  Act,  No.  36  of  2003  to  introduce  a
“Geographical  Indications  Registration  System”  to  provide  for  the  registration  and
administration  of  geographical  indications  and  matters  relating  thereto.
