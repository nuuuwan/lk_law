AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE CENTRAL BANK OF
SRI LANKA;  FOR  THE  REPEAL  OF  THE MONETARY  LAW  ACT
(CHAPTER 422);  AND  TO  PROVIDE  FOR  MATTERS  CONNECTED
THEREWITH OR INCIDENTAL THERETO.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1.

(1)  This  Act  may  be  cited  as  the  Central  Bank  of
Sri Lanka Act, No.    of 2023 and shall come into operation
on such date as the Minister may appoint by Order published
in the Gazette (in this Act referred to as the “appointed date”).

(in  this  Act  referred  to  as  the  “Central  Bank”)  to  be  an
