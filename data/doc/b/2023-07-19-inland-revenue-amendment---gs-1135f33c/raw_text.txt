STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2:  This  clause  amends  the  First  Schedule  of  the  Inland  Revenue  Act,  No. 24  of
2017  and  the  legal  effect  of  that  Schedule  as  amended  is  to  increase  the  income  tax  rate
applicable  to  the  Employees  Trust  Fund,  an  approved  provident  or  pension  fund,  or  an
approved  termination  fund  up  to  30%  and  the  income  tax  rate  applicable  to  the  funds
which  effectively  participate  in  the  process  of  domestic  debt  optimization  to  continue
as  14%.
