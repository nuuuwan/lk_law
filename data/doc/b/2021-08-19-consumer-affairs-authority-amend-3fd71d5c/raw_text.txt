STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

Clause  2  :   This clause amends section 60 of the Consumer Affairs Authority
Act, No.9 of 2003, and the legal effect of the section as amended is to introduce a new
penalty  in  respect  of  offences  committed  under  subsection  (4A)  of  section  60  by
contravening any provisions of subsection (5) of section 20 of the said Act.
