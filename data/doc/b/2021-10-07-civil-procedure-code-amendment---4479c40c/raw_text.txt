STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause    2  :  This  clause  amends  section  88  of  the  Civil  Procedure  Code  (Chapter  101)
and  the  legal  effect  of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  provide  for  an  appeal  to  the  Provincial
High Court with leave obtained first in case of an order setting aside or refusing to set aside
the  judgment  entered  upon  default.
