STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

Clause 2: This clause replaces the long title to the Fauna and Flora Protection Ordinance
(Chapter 469) (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and the legal effect of the
amendment is to expand the scope of the principal enactment.

Clause 3: This clause amends section 71 of the principal enactment and the legal effect as
amended is to empower the Minister to make regulations in order to give effect to the Convention
on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES).
