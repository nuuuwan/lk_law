Clause  2:  This  clause  amends  the  First  Schedule  to  the  Social  Security  Contribution
Levy  Act,  No.  25  of  2022  in  order  to  add  items  25,  26,  27,  28  and  29  as  exempted  articles
under  PART  IA,  and  to  reward  item  1  under  PART  II  for  the  avoidance  of  doubt  and  the
avoidance  of  electricity  price  variations  due  to  the  Social  Security  Contribution  Levy  and
to  add  item  24  as  an  exempted  service  under  PART  II.
