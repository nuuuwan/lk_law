AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE NATIONAL

COMMISSION FOR THE UNITED NATIONS EDUCATIONAL, SCIENTIFIC

AND CULTURAL ORGANIZATION (UNESCO); AND FOR MATTERS

CONNECTED THEREWITH OR INCIDENTAL THERETO.
