STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2  :  This  clause  replaces  section  32  of  the    Registration  of  Documents  Ordinance
(Chapter  117)  (hereinafter  referred  to  as  the  “principal  enactment”)  and  the  legal  effect  of
the  section  as  amended  is  to  enhance  the  scope  of  provisions  relating  to  presentation  of  a
caveat  for  registration  and  to  define  certain  terms.

Clause  3  :  This  clause  amends  section  33  of  the  principal  enactment  and  the  legal  effect
of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  enhance  the  scope  of  this  section  relating  to  withdrawal  and
cancellation  of  a  caveat.

Clause  4  :  This  clause  amends  section  50  of  the  principal  enactment  and  the  legal  effect

of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  make  provisions  for  clarity.

Clause  5  :  This  clause  amends  the  Second  Schedule  to  the  principal  enactment  and  is

consequential  to  the  amendments  made  by  the  above  clauses.
