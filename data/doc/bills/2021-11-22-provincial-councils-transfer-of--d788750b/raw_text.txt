STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

Clause 2: This clause amends section 2 of the Provincial Councils (Transfer of Stamp Duty)
Act, No. 13 of 2011 and the legal effect of the section as amended is to provide for refunding any
stamp duty overpaid or erroneously paid.
