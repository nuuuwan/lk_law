SECURED TRANSACTIONS

A

BILL

to provide for an institutional framework for the regulation of Secured 
Transactions  in  movable  property  by  the  establishment  of  the  Secured 
Transactions  Registration  Authority;  to  set  out  the  powers  and  duties 
of such Authority; to provide for the determining of priorities amongst 
creditors  having  competing  security  rights  in  the  movable  property  of 
their  debtors;  to  set  out  the  rights  and  duties  of  parties  to  security 
agreements; to provide for the establishment and maintenance of a registry 
of security rights in movable property and for matters connected therewith 
or incidental thereto
