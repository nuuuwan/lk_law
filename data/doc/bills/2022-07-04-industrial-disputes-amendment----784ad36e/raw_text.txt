STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

Clause 2: This clause amends section 46 of the Industrial Disputes Act (Chapter 131) and
the legal effect of the section as amended is to provide for representation of any party to any
proceeding  under  the  said  Act  by  an  Attorney-at-law  or  a  representative  as  specified  in  that
section.
