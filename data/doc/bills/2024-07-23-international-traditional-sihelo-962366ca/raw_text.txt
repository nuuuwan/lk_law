AN  ACT  TO  INCORPORATE  THE  INTERNATIONAL TRADITIONAL
SIHELOGY COUNCIL (ITSC)

WHEREAS  a  Council  named  and  known  as  the
“International  Traditional  Sihelogy  Council  (ITSC)”,  has
heretofore been established in Sri Lanka for the purpose of
effectually carrying out its objects and transacting all matters
connected  with  the  said  Council  according  to  the  rules
agreed to, by its members:

Preamble

AND  WHEREAS  the  said  Council  has  heretofore
successfully carried out and transacted the several objects
and matters for which it was established and has applied to
be  incorporated  and  it  will  be  for  the  public  advantage  to
grant  such  application:

BE it therefore enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

Interpretation

5

10

“Sihelogy” means, a scientific way of living belongs
to the four main great tribes of sivhela Yakka,
Deva, Naga and Raksha from which it was
originated  and  continued  since  the  time
immemorial  including  sivhela  or  sihela
medicine,  nursing,  farming,  martial  arts,
performing  arts,  food  culture,  educational
concepts, beauty culture, physio therapy and
oil  therapy,  traditional  acupuncture,  wood
carving and tree arts and pharmacology.
