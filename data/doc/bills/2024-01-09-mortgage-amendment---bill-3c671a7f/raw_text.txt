STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

Clause  2  :  This  clause  inserts  the  new  section  68A  in  the  Mortgage Act  (Chapter  89),
and  the  legal  effect  of  that  section  is  to  limit  the  applicability  of  Part  III  of  the  Mortgage

Act,  in  relation  to  movable  property  upon  coming  into  operation  of  the  Secured

Transactions Act,  No.      of  2023.
